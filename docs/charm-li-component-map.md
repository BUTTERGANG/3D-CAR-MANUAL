## PRIORITY ORDER FOR 3D MODEL DATA
1. **Engine, Cooling and Exhaust** — highest maintenance value, most reference material
2. **Brakes and Traction Control** — safety-critical, frequent service items
3. **Steering and Suspension** — common wear items, visual reference abundant
4. **Transmission and Drivetrain** — major repair zone
5. **Starting and Charging** — battery, alternator, starter (simple geometry)
6. **HVAC** — accessible components under hood
7. **Body and Frame** — exterior shell (use purchased model as base)
8. **Lighting** — exterior lights (simple geometry, high visual value)
